Timeline of Steve Jobes

  1. 1955 - Steve Jobes was born.
  2. 1972 - Graduated from Homestead High School in Cupertino, Calif., and enrolled at Reed College in Portland.
  3. 1974 - Began a brief stint as an engineer at Atari.
  4. 1976 - Co-founded Apple Computer with Wozniak and Ronald Wayne
  5. 1997 - Became CEO, initially as the de facto chief, then as interim chief in September.
  6. 1998 - Introduced the iMac, which became commercially available in August.
  7. 2005 - Introduced iPod Nano
  8. 2007 - Introduced the iPhone
  9. 2008 - Introduced the App Store as an update to iTunes. Also introduced the MacBook Air.
  10. 2011 - Resigned from Apple CEO post; became chairman.
  11. 2011 - Died in Palo Alto, California, United States because of Pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor.
